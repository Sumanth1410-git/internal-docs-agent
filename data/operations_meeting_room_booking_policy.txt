MEETING ROOM BOOKING POLICY 2025

Booking Rules:
- Book maximum 7 days in advance
- Minimum 2-hour slots, maximum 8 hours
- Cancel at least 2 hours before meeting
- Clean up after use required

Room Capacities:
- Conference A: 20 people, has projector and whiteboard
- Conference B: 12 people, video conferencing enabled  
- Training Room: 30 people, presentation equipment
- Phone Booths: 1-2 people, soundproof for calls

Booking Process: Use company portal or Outlook calendar integration
Priority: Client meetings > team meetings > training sessions
Contact facilities@company.com for technical issues.
- No food or drinks in meeting rooms except water
- Report any damages immediately
