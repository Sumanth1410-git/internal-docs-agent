MANDATORY TRAINING REQUIREMENTS 2025

All Employees:
- Information Security Awareness (Annual)
- Workplace Harassment Prevention (Annual)
- Code of Conduct Refresh (Biennial)
- Emergency Procedures (Annual)

Role-Specific Requirements:
Managers:
- Leadership Development (Annual)
- Performance Management Training (Biennial)
- Budget Management Basics (As needed)

IT Staff:
- Cybersecurity Advanced Training (Quarterly)
- Data Protection Protocols (Biannual)

Sales Team:
- Customer Data Privacy (Quarterly)
- Sales Ethics Training (Annual)

Deadlines: Complete by December 31st annually
Tracking: HR Learning Management System
Non-compliance: May affect performance reviews
- Contact HR for assistance with training modules
- Additional resources available on the company intranet
