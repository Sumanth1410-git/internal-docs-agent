EMPLOYEE CODE OF CONDUCT 2025

Core Values:
- Integrity in all business dealings
- Respect for colleagues and clients
- Accountability for actions and decisions
- Excellence in work quality

Prohibited Behaviors:
- Discrimination or harassment
- Conflicts of interest
- Misuse of company resources
- Sharing confidential information

Reporting Process:
- Supervisor or HR for general issues
- Anonymous hotline: 1-800-ETHICS
- Online portal: ethics.company.com
- No retaliation policy enforced

Social Media Guidelines:
- Don't share confidential company information
- Use disclaimers when posting about work
- Maintain professional image online
- Report cyberbullying or harassment
- Personal accounts should not imply official company endorsement
- Follow copyright and trademark laws
