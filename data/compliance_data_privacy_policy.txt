DATA PRIVACY & PROTECTION POLICY 2025

Employee Responsibilities:
- Protect customer and company data at all times
- Use strong passwords and enable 2FA
- Report data breaches immediately
- Complete annual privacy training

Data Classification:
- Public: Company marketing materials
- Internal: Employee directories, policies
- Confidential: Financial data, customer info
- Restricted: Legal documents, strategic plans

Data Handling Rules:
- No personal devices for confidential data
- Encrypt all data in transit and at rest
- Regular backups with 30-day retention
- Secure disposal of physical documents

Violations: May result in disciplinary action including termination
Contact: privacy@company.com for questions
Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Employee Benefits:
- Health insurance, retirement plans, paid time off
