REMOTE WORK COLLABORATION BEST PRACTICES 2025

- Respond to messages within 4 hours during business hours
- Use video calls for important discussions
- Update calendar with availability status
- Weekly team check-ins mandatory

Meeting Etiquette:
- Mute when not speaking
- Professional background required
- Test technology before important meetings
- Share screens appropriately

Productivity Expectations:
- Core hours: 10 AM - 3 PM in your timezone
- Deliver on commitments and deadlines  
- Proactive communication about blockers
- Regular status updates to manager

Work-Life Balance:
- Respect colleagues' time zones
- Avoid after-hours messages unless urgent
- Take proper lunch breaks
- Use vacation time appropriately
- Encourage team members to disconnect after work hours
- Mental health days available upon request
